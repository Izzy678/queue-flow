
You are a senior product designer and frontend engineer.

Design and implement a world-class SaaS landing page for a Queue Management System called QueueFlow.

The design quality should feel comparable to Stripe, Linear, Intercom, and Calendly.

Goals:

* Immediately communicate reduced waiting times and improved customer experience.
* Feel premium, modern, trustworthy, and enterprise-ready.
* Convert visitors into demo bookings or signups.

Technology:

* Next.js
* Tailwind CSS
* Framer Motion
* Shadcn UI

Design Style:

* Modern SaaS aesthetic
* Dark mode by default
* Glassmorphism where appropriate
* Soft gradients
* Excellent spacing and typography
* Smooth micro-interactions
* Mobile responsive

Page Structure:

1. Navbar

* Logo
* Features
* Solutions
* Pricing
* Contact
* Login button
* Get Started CTA

2. Hero Section

* Headline focused on eliminating customer waiting frustration
* Supporting copy
* Primary CTA
* Secondary CTA
* Interactive dashboard mockup showing:

  * Active queues
  * Average wait time
  * Current ticket number
  * Customers served today
* Floating animated queue ticket cards
* Animated statistics counters

3. Trusted By Section

* Placeholder company logos

4. Features Section

* Digital ticket issuance
* Real-time queue tracking
* SMS/email notifications
* Multi-branch management
* Analytics dashboard
* Customer feedback collection

5. How It Works

* Join Queue
* Wait Remotely
* Receive Notification
* Get Served

6. Product Showcase

* Large dashboard preview
* Animated charts
* Queue monitoring interface
* Branch management view

7. Benefits Section

* Reduced congestion
* Better customer satisfaction
* Faster service delivery
* Actionable analytics

8. Testimonials

9. Pricing

10. FAQ

11. Footer

Animations:

* Scroll reveal animations
* Staggered card animations
* Hover effects
* Floating dashboard elements
* Smooth page transitions
* Count-up statistics
* Subtle parallax effects

Avoid:

* Generic bootstrap appearance
* Corporate-looking templates
* Excessive colors
* Overly rounded childish designs

The final result should look like a modern startup that recently raised venture capital and hired an elite product design team.
